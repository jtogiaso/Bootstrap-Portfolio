About Me

My very first video game was Shadow of the Colossus. A magnificent adventure that follows a nameless swordsman vying to ressurect his deceased love by embarking upon the impossible quest to slay 16 monster collosi. Captivated by the gripping story, the elegant beasts, and the grougeous landscape, I knew there had to be more that could offer this same realm of escape and thrill.

Video gaming emerses users in impossible worlds, much better than any other medium available. Games like Assassin's Creed have brought historical events to the present and has given players the opportunity to experience first hand the thrills of many events. With this aspect in mind, I want to create a style of education that gears towards a regiment more suitable for us tactile/kinestetic learners. Taking this coding boot camp is the first step to cultivating/developing the skills that I need in order to achieve my dream.
